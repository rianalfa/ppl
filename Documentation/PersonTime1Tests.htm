Conclusions: The Normal approximation and Poisson approximations agree with those from other sources.  I was unable to find an example of the Rothman, Greenland confidence limits done by exactly the same method, but they appear to agree fairly closely with the other two, and we will continue to search for examples.  There appears to be a fair amount of diversity among results of different methods and software packages.  It should be safe to use the Normal for large samples and the Byar for small ones, however.  A warning has been included in the output page for the OpenEpi module.
