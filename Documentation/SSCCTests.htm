Testing by Andy Dean.  .

 

Conclusions: OpenEpi sample size programs round results UP rather than to the nearest integer, since it is difficult to find 0.1 person to participate in a study.  Agreement takes this into account.  Differences in S-3 should be investigated, but are not serious for practical work. 

 

These results are sufficient to remove the BETA labels from the programs, although further testing and comparison of important results with those from other programs is always in order.  Please send us results of further testing so that we can add them to this list.
